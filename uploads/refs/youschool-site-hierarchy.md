# youschool.fr — page hierarchy

The public site is a WordPress organized around 3 page types.

## Main Pages (PP)

Individual training pages. The root page of each formation.

Examples: `/cap-patisserie/`, `/cap-cuisine/`, `/cap-esthetique-cosmetique/`

These are the formations listed inside the mega-menu sector columns.

## Secondary Pages

Sub-pages attached to each Main Page. They cover a specific aspect of the training.

URL pattern: `/[formation-slug]/[page]/` or `/[formation-slug]/formation/[page]/` (inconsistent across formations)

Recurring secondary pages: Programme, Alternance, Examen, Stages, Financement CPF, Débouchés, Avis élèves.

## Global Pages

Pages that concern YouSchool as a school, not tied to any specific training.

Examples: homepage, sector indexes (`/formation-restauration/`), `/financement/`, `/alternance/`, `/contact/`, blog, legal pages.
